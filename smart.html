SMART criteria

Specific: non technical overview of what we want to do.

Measurable: breaking your goal down into measurable elements.

Archievable:a target must be achievable.

Realistic:Although feasible, the goal may not be realistic. To determine whether the goal is realistic is necessary to check: -who is the person in charge to make it happen. -If the person has the skills required to make it happen. -if there is money to finance the project.

Timely:In an objective that meets this requirement are defined lead times. It is also necessary to define the intermediate times which define the status of the project.
